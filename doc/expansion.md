`smsh` Expansion

In `smsh`, expansions are strictly explicit, and occur within
braces, `{}`. 


Expansion Types:
    {}          User-Defeind Variable Expansion
    e{}         Environment Variable Expansion
    !{}         Subshell Expansion

Separation Modifiers:
    {}          [None] Split by word
    {}s=":"     Arbitrary Separators
    {}L         Split by line
